Questions & Philosophy

What makes Atrest different from other fasting apps?
Atrest is designed for serious fasting, not motivation. It avoids streaks, badges, and gamification. Instead, it offers a quiet, premium environment where your discipline becomes a living memory in the form of a forest.
Is this app for Christians only?
No. Atrest is for anyone who takes fasting seriously and prefers a calm, aesthetic experience. Some features, like Vigil Mode, are designed for people who fast during specific sacred periods, but the core app is neutral and non-religious.
Does the forest gamify fasting?
No. The forest is a visual record of continuity, not a game. There are no rewards, points, or streak mechanics. The forest grows as a quiet reflection of your practice, not as a way to pressure you.
How does Vigil Mode work?
Vigil Mode is an optional layer for people who fast during periods like Lent, Advent, or the Apostles’ Fast. It adds quiet scriptural reflections without changing the core tracking experience. It is completely optional and can be turned off at any time.
Is my data private?
Yes. Atrest is designed with privacy in mind. Your fasting history is stored securely and is never sold. Only the minimum necessary data is collected to provide the core experience.
Will Atrest stay quiet and minimal?
Yes. Atrest is built on a clear doctrine: no noise, no gamification, no motivational tricks. The product will remain quiet, serious, and aesthetic as it evolves.
